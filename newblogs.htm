INTRODUCTION ON TECHNOLOGY

Basically, technology in real life refers to the positive revolution to the standard of learning. It indicates to the applied science. Term technology is very much wide, it has no boundaries and limitations. Technology is a dyamic process because it keeps on growing and improving as our demands and desires never end it keeps on changing. So with the flow of our needs and desires the technology also needs to be changed. No one can stop the term technology. Technology helps to simplify our daily tasks..... Some of the great revolutions in technology can include the creation of smartphones, computers, robots and so on. We also use technology to expose our abilities/capabilities.

HOW TECHNOLOGY HAS AFFECTED OUR LIVING?

Obviously, Technologies that we use in our daily life has adverse affect in our living. Technology has the positive as well as negative impact in our daily life. All the achievement regarding the technology are so real and fascinating. In past, the stuffs that took us few weeks or month to accomplish has now transfigured into just few hours or minutes. For example, in the past age our ancestors used to send their message to their respective persons via letter which would take weeks or even a month to deliver but now at present if we desire to send the message to anyone in the world we can simply send it in few seonds by sitting in a corner via internet. ....This reflects the development of technology. In present context technology has become one of the most powerful weapon because technologies are implemented or applicable in various sectors. Even in the field of education also technology has been concerned as the mandatory tool for effective and systematic learning. Nowadays in each and every schools, colleges and universities there exhibts the use of computer, even the entire class are technically based, as the classes are organized or taught through the assist of computers and projectors. Besides this in several restaurants, robots are kept for serving the dishes to the customers. On the other hand the negative impacts of technology are also very much severe topic. Because of the development of technology, human beings have become so introvert and unfriendly with each other. For example; due to the invention of smartphones, nowadays everyone has their mobile phones in their hand and they are so busy in their phones that they lack in giving time to their families or friends. Those person who are far are brought close and those who are nearby are pretended to be far, in both cases technology is the prime source.
